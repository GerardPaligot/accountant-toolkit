# Sources consulted and verified (April 2026)

This document lists the sources used to build the `impots-fr` skill. To be updated each year after the loi de finances is published and the BOFiP is refreshed.

## Official (authoritative)

### Codes and laws
- **Code Général des Impôts (CGI)** - Legifrance
  - Art. 194 to 197: quotient familial, parts
  - Art. 156: deductions from global income, déficit foncier
  - Art. 157 bis: seniors allowance
  - Art. 81 quater: overtime hours
  - Art. 81-36°: student jobs < 26 years
  - Art. 81 bis: apprentices
  - Art. 150 U: real-estate capital gains
  - Art. 150 VH bis: digital-asset capital gains (€305 threshold)
  - Art. 150 VB bis: reintegration of LMNP depreciation (LF 2025)
  - Art. 155 B: inbound-expatriate regime
  - Art. 163-0 A: quotient system
  - Art. 163 quatervicies: PER, ceilings
  - Art. 199 sexdecies: home employment
  - Art. 199 octodecies: compensatory benefit (prestation compensatoire)
  - Art. 199 novovicies: Denormandie
  - Art. 199 tervicies: Malraux
  - Art. 199 tricies: Loc'Avantages
  - Art. 199 terdecies-0 A: IR-PME/FIP/FCPI
  - Art. 199 unvicies: SOFICA
  - Art. 199 undecies B / C: Girardin industriel / social housing
  - Art. 199 decies H: forestry investment
  - Art. 199 quater B: union dues (credit)
  - Art. 199 quater F: tuition fees
  - Art. 200: donations to charities
  - Art. 200-1 ter: Coluche donations
  - Art. 200-3: donations to political parties
  - Art. 200-0 A: global niches cap €10,000 / €18,000
  - Art. 200 A: PFU 30% (31.4% from 2026)
  - Art. 200 quater B: childcare < 6 years
  - Art. 200 quater C: charging point (removed 01/01/2026)
  - Art. 200 sexdecies A: first press-subscription tax credit
  - Art. 154 bis: Madelin prévoyance/retirement TNS
  - Art. 224: CDHR
  - Art. 979: IFI capping

- **Livre des Procédures Fiscales (LPF)**
  - Art. L64, L64 A: abuse of law (abus de droit)
  - Art. L169: reassessment period (3 years / 10 years)

- **Loi de finances 2026** (adopted late 2025):
  - Scale revaluation +0.9%
  - Individualised PAS rate by default
  - CDHR renewed
  - PER: ceiling carry-forward 3 -> 5 years, end of deduction after age 70
  - Déficit foncier doubling extended until 31/12/2027
  - Coluche donations: 75% ceiling raised to €2,000 (donations >= 14/10/2025)
  - Jeanbrun: new rental scheme
  - Relance logement: 3 years, multi-unit buildings
  - Charging point: removed 01/01/2026
  - CSG on capital: 9.2% -> 10.6% (PFU 31.4%)

- **Loi de finances 2025** and **loi Le Meur** (19/11/2024):
  - LMNP reform: reintegration of depreciation into the capital gain
  - Unclassified tourist lettings: micro-BIC threshold €15,000, 30% allowance
  - Classified tourist lettings: 50% allowance
